Roadmap

  • Concept & Vision

    The idea was simple: Make the cutest, most chaotic catgirl token Solana has ever seen. Nekonomi blends waifu charm, meme culture, and degen spirit. She’s more than a mascot — she’s the main character. We built her world from the paws up, complete with lore, cats, and chaos. Goals: Build a strong identity, introduce her cat crew, and create something that lives beyond the chart.

  • Build & Launch

    We brought Nekonomi to life with original art, comics, and a low-cap presale for early believers. Tokenomics were locked, airdrops kicked off, and lore dropped weekly. This wasn't just a launch — it was an entrance. Launched: Token presale, comics, airdrop #1, Telegram & X, and her first missions: Mission Catpossible & Enchanted Woods.

  • Growth & Expansion

    Nekonomi is growing. Next up: DEX listings, merch drops, airdrop #2, and more comics. The community drives the chaos — memes, fanart, and lore will fuel the next leg up. Coming: Raydium launch, weekly content, exclusive merch, creative contests, XP leaderboard, and a sneak peek at the first Nekonomi minigame.

  • Legitimacy & Listings

    As Nekonomi grows, so does the need for stability. We're focused on making her a recognizable, sustainable asset across Solana and beyond. That means transparency, consistent updates, and the big leap: centralized exchanges. Next Steps: Contract audit, DEX tracking tools, strategic CEX outreach, liquidity expansion, and community governance features for long-term legitimacy.

What are the token allocations?

+

The $NEKONOMI token is distributed with community and sustainability in mind. 20% is allocated to the presale, giving early supporters access to the project. 10% is reserved for a community airdrop, rewarding early engagement. To ensure long-term alignment, 20% is allocated to the team, locked securely on Streamflow. An additional 25% goes toward marketing, with 20% of that also locked for 6 months on Streamflow to support strategic growth. 10% will be burned, increasing scarcity. 10% is allocated to liquidity at launch via Raydium, and 5% is set aside for ongoing development.
